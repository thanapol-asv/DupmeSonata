----- How To Play -----

Pair up with an opponent either by challenging one of them directly or accepting their challenges.
Once you are paired up with the opponent, you and your opponent would be randomly selected to go first.
In case you are the first one to go: You have 10 seconds to create a pattern on the piano as you desire. Then your opponent would have 20 seconds trying to duplicate your pattern. The more accurate the following pattern, the more accumulated point he/she would receive.
In case you are the second player: You have to wait for 10 seconds and try to memorize your opponent’s piano pattern. Then you would have 20 seconds trying to duplicate your opponent’s pattern. The more accurate the following pattern, the more accumulated point you would receive.
You and your opponent would take turns to create piano patterns and try to copy the other player patterns.
If you make a wrong guess, don't worry and move on to the next note!
Both of you would have 2 chances to create your own piano patterns and so does your opponent.
You have 3 hints per game, which will play the next key for you, so play on!
